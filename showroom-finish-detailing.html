About the Service

What is Showroom Finish Detailing?

Showroom finish detailing is a comprehensive, multi-step car care process that goes far beyond a regular car wash. At DRK Stickers, we restore your vehicle's paint, correct surface imperfections, eliminate interior odours, and apply protective coatings — leaving your car looking exactly as it did the day it left the showroom.

Coimbatore's intense heat, dusty roads, and monsoon rains are especially harsh on your car's paint and interior. Our professional auto detailing process is the most effective way to restore and protect your vehicle from all of these.

Whether you drive a hatchback, sedan, SUV, or luxury car — our expert technicians treat every vehicle with the same meticulous care and attention to detail.

Our Detailing Process

8-Step Showroom Method

Every detail session follows our proven professional process — no shortcuts, no rushing. Each step builds on the last to achieve a true showroom-quality result.

01
Step 01

Pre-Wash & Foam Bath

High-pressure pre-rinse followed by thick foam application to lift surface contaminants before any physical contact — preventing paint scratches.

02
Step 02

Two-Bucket Hand Wash

pH-balanced shampoo and a microfibre wash mitt — top to bottom, panel by panel. Wheels and tyres cleaned separately with dedicated wheel cleaner.

03
Step 03

Clay Bar Decontamination

Professional clay bar treatment removes embedded tar, brake dust, and industrial fallout that washing alone cannot reach — leaving paint smooth as glass.

04
Step 04

Paint Correction & Polish

Machine polisher removes swirl marks, fine scratches, water spots, and oxidation — restoring your paint's original depth, clarity, and gloss.

05
Step 05

Wax / Sealant Protection

High-quality carnauba wax or synthetic sealant locks in the shine and shields paint from UV rays, dust, bird droppings, and Coimbatore's monsoon rain.

06
Step 06

Interior Deep Clean

Full vacuum of seats, carpets, and boot. Upholstery shampooing, dashboard and door panel wipe-down with appropriate conditioners.

07
Step 07

Glass & Trim Treatment

All windows streak-free inside and out. Exterior plastic trim restored with conditioner for a deep, rich finish that lasts.

08
Step 08

Tyre Dress & Final Inspection

Tyres dressed for a deep black finish. Full inspection under bright light — every panel checked before we hand your car back.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

A full showroom finish detail typically takes 4 to 8 hours depending on your car's size and current condition. We recommend dropping your car in the morning so it's ready by evening. For very neglected vehicles, we may recommend booking an overnight session.

We recommend a full detail every 4 to 6 months. Coimbatore's heat, dust, and monsoon rains are particularly damaging to paint and interior surfaces. Regular detailing protects your car's value and keeps it looking its best year-round.

Paint correction and polishing will remove or significantly reduce surface-level swirl marks, fine scratches, and water spots. Deep scratches that penetrate through the clear coat down to the base coat may require a paint touch-up service. Our technicians will assess your car and advise you honestly.

A regular car wash cleans the surface — it removes loose dirt and dust. Professional detailing corrects, restores, and protects every part of your vehicle. It includes paint correction, clay bar treatment, wax protection, interior deep cleaning, and more. The result is a car that doesn't just look clean — it looks showroom-new.

Clay bar treatment removes microscopic contaminants — tar, brake dust, industrial fallout — that are bonded to the paint surface and cannot be removed by washing. Without clay bar decontamination, your paint will feel slightly rough to the touch. After clay bar treatment, paint feels perfectly smooth and wax or sealant bonds far better.

Carnauba wax provides a warm, deep glow and lasts around 3 to 6 months. Ceramic coating is a semi-permanent, nano-technology based protective layer that bonds to the paint and can last 1 to 5 years. It provides superior protection against UV, chemical stains, and water. Our team can advise which option suits your car and budget best.

Yes — we detail all types of vehicles including hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, MUVs, and luxury/premium cars. All cars receive the same meticulous care and professional process regardless of make or model.
