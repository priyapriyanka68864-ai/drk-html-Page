Understanding the service

What Is UV Protection Film for Windows?

UV protection film for windows is a thin, precision-engineered layer applied directly onto glass surfaces — car windows, home panes, office partitions, and more. It is designed to block ultraviolet (UV-A and UV-B) rays and solar infrared heat that pass through ordinary glass every single day.

Unlike standard window tint, an anti-fade UV protection layer targets the root cause of colour fading and heat build-up. It works silently — protecting your car dashboard, leather seats, wooden furniture, fabric upholstery, flooring, and even your skin — without altering your view or blocking natural light.

At DRK Stickers, Coimbatore, we use high-performance UV-blocking film that is scratch-resistant, bubble-free, and built to last in India's intense tropical climate.

Technology & process

How Our UV Protection Layer Works

1

UV Absorption Layer

The inner layer captures and neutralises UV-A and UV-B rays before they penetrate glass — blocking up to 99% of harmful ultraviolet radiation. This is the core anti-fade, UV-blocking window film technology.

2

Infrared Heat Rejection Layer

Solar infrared energy is the primary cause of heat build-up inside your car or room. This layer reflects IR rays, reducing interior temperatures and cutting AC dependency — a key feature of solar heat rejection film.

3

Scratch-Resistant Hardcoat

The outermost surface is finished with a hardcoat that resists scratches, dust abrasion, and daily wear — keeping the UV film for car and home glass looking crystal-clear for years.

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will UV protection film make my windows too dark?
Not necessarily. We offer clear and lightly tinted UV films that block UV rays without noticeably darkening your glass. You choose the tint level that works best for your needs and preferences.
How long does the UV protection film last?
With professional installation and basic care, our UV protection films typically last between 5 to 10 years. We use scratch-resistant, fade-proof film materials for maximum longevity in India's climate.
Can UV film be applied to already-tinted windows?
In most cases, yes. Our team will assess your existing glass and advise on the best approach — whether that involves removing old film first or applying directly over it.
Does UV film affect visibility from inside?
No. Our films are designed for optical clarity. Clear UV films are virtually invisible, and even tinted variants maintain excellent outward visibility from inside the car or room.
How long does installation take?
For a standard car, installation takes 2–4 hours. For home or office glass, it depends on the number of windows and total glass area. We provide a time estimate during consultation.
Is car window UV film legal in India?
Yes, provided the film meets the Visible Light Transmission (VLT) standards set by the Motor Vehicles Act. Our team ensures all car window films fully comply with legal requirements.
Will the film peel or bubble over time?
Not when professionally installed. DRK Stickers uses proper surface preparation and high-quality adhesive films. Peeling and bubbling are signs of poor installation or low-grade film — both of which we avoid entirely.
