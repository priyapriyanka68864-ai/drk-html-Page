Understanding the Service
What is Privacy
Window Tinting?

Privacy window tinting is the application of a specially designed dark-tinted film to your car's windows — reducing visibility from outside into your cabin while maintaining clear outward visibility for the driver and passengers.

Unlike basic sun films that focus on heat rejection, privacy window tinting uses darker shade films to make it significantly harder for people outside to see into your car — giving you security and personal space without compromising your view of the road.

At DRK Stickers, our window tinting service in Coimbatore combines privacy performance with UV protection, heat rejection and a premium aesthetic finish.

✅ Yes — Window Tinting is Legal in Tamil Nadu

Window tinting is fully legal in Tamil Nadu and Coimbatore — provided the film meets the Visible Light Transmission (VLT) requirements set under the Motor Vehicles Act. At DRK Stickers, we only install legally compliant grades and guide every customer before installation.
