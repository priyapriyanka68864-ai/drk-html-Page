The problem
Why Coimbatore cars need glass treatment

Extreme cabin heat

Car glass acts as a greenhouse. A parked cabin in Coimbatore's summer can exceed 60°C. Even while driving, untreated glass amplifies solar heat making AC work twice as hard.

Harmful UV exposure

Standard car glass blocks very little UV radiation. Prolonged exposure through windows causes cumulative skin damage — equivalent to sun exposure without sunscreen.

Interior fading & cracking

UV radiation bleaches dashboards, fades seat upholstery, and cracks leather and vinyl. This damage happens silently every day without a UV blocking film in place.

Glare & driver fatigue

Intense sun glare on Coimbatore's busy roads causes eye strain, reduces reaction time, and makes driving genuinely unsafe — especially on east-west roads in the morning.

Numbers decoded
Understanding film performance specs
UV%

UV rejection

% of ultraviolet rays blocked. Protects skin and prevents dashboard/seat fading. Look for 99%+.

VLT

Visible light transmission

% of visible light passing through. Higher = lighter, more natural-looking. Indian law requires 70%+ front windshield, 50%+ front side.

TSER

Total solar energy rejection

The most complete heat measure — covers all solar energy types. 60%+ TSER means premium performance. Higher is better.

IRR

Infrared rejection

% of infrared heat blocked — the heat you physically feel. Nano-ceramic and crystalline films achieve 95–99% IRR.

RTO compliance
CMVR Rule 100(2) compliant

Is your window film RTO-legal?

Indian law under CMVR Rule 100(2) and IS: 2553 (Part 2) — 1992 mandates minimum visible light transmission levels for car glass. DRK Stickers only recommends and installs films that keep your vehicle fully compliant — every customer is guided on legal film choices before any work begins.

Questions answered
Frequently asked questions
How much does window film installation cost in Coimbatore?
Entry-level carbon or dyed films start from ₹2,500–₹5,000. Ceramic and nano-ceramic films range from ₹6,000–₹15,000. Full-car 3M Crystalline or V-KOOL installations range from ₹18,000–₹35,000. Contact DRK Stickers for a free, vehicle-specific quote.
Will a clear UV blocking film actually keep my car cooler?
Yes — significantly. Premium ceramic and nano-ceramic films block infrared radiation, which is the heat component of sunlight. Quality films reduce cabin temperature by 8–15°C and dramatically reduce the time your AC takes to cool the cabin — even in Coimbatore's peak summer temperatures.
Is window film legal in India?
Yes, as long as it meets CMVR VLT requirements: 70% or above for front windshields and 50% or above for front side windows. DRK Stickers only installs RTO-compliant films and guides every customer on legal film choices before any work begins.
Will window film interfere with my GPS, mobile, FASTag, or 5G signal?
No — ceramic, nano-ceramic, and multi-layer optical films like 3M Crystalline are fully metal-free and signal-safe. GPS, 4G, 5G, Bluetooth, and FASTag (National Electronic Toll Collection) all work without any interference after installation.
How long does installation take?
A full-car window film installation typically takes 3–5 hours depending on vehicle size and film type. We advise keeping windows rolled up for 24–48 hours after installation for full adhesive curing.
How long does quality window film last?
Premium ceramic and crystalline films from 3M, LLumar, and V-KOOL last 7–12 years with proper care. Avoid ammonia-based window cleaners on filmed glass as these degrade the adhesive over time. Use a microfibre cloth and mild soap instead.
Can you remove my old, bubbling film and replace it?
Absolutely — this is one of our most commonly requested services. We professionally remove existing film including stubborn adhesive residue, prepare the glass, and install a fresh high-performance replacement from 3M, LLumar, Garware, or V-KOOL.
