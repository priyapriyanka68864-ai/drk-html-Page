The Science — Made Simple
What is Graphene Coating
— and How Does it Work?

Graphene is a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a honeycomb lattice — one atom thick, 200 times stronger than steel, incredibly flexible and highly thermally conductive. It won the Nobel Prize in 2010 for its extraordinary material properties.

Graphene coating takes the proven chemistry of ceramic coating (SiO2) and enhances it by infusing graphene oxide particles into the ceramic base — creating a hybrid nano-coating that chemically bonds with your car's paint and delivers significantly better performance in every category.

The Simple Analogy
🔵
Ceramic Coating = A hardened glass shield bonded to your paint
⚫
Graphene Coating = That same glass shield — reinforced with microscopic carbon mesh woven through it. Stronger. Smarter. Longer lasting.
🧬 The Chemistry

Standard ceramic coating = SiO2 (Silicon Dioxide) only. Graphene coating = SiO2 + Graphene Oxide (rGO). The graphene oxide molecules fill the gaps in the ceramic matrix — creating a denser, more heat-conductive and chemically resistant protective layer.

⚡ Anti-Static Property

Graphene is electrically conductive. This reduces the static charge on your car's paint — actively repelling dust particles that would normally be attracted electrostatically to the surface. No other coating technology does this.

🌡️ Thermal Conductivity

Graphene distributes and dissipates surface heat faster than ceramic alone. This prevents heat build-up on your panels that causes water spots and coating stress — especially critical in Coimbatore's climate where panels reach 65–70°C in summer.

Our Method
Graphene Coating
Application Process

Graphene coating demands precise, multi-step preparation for the graphene oxide to bond correctly. At DRK Stickers, every step is executed with professional precision — no shortcuts.

01
Free Paint Inspection & Consultation

Thorough paint assessment under studio lighting — documenting all swirl marks, scratches, water spots and defects. We discuss findings with you and recommend the right graphene package for your car's condition and ownership plan. Free of charge.

02
Foam Pre-Wash & Full Decontamination

Complete foam pre-wash followed by thorough hand wash removes all surface dirt. Iron fallout remover is applied to dissolve brake dust and metallic particles embedded in the paint — a critical step before graphene application.

03
Clay Bar Paint Decontamination

Full clay bar treatment removes all remaining embedded contaminants — industrial fallout, road tar, tree sap residue and bonded particles that washing alone cannot remove. Ensures graphene coating bonds to clean, bare paint only.

04
Multi-Stage Paint Correction

Professional dual-action polishers with compound grades matched to your paint remove all swirl marks, light scratches and surface defects. Since graphene coating permanently locks in paint condition, correction-level perfection before application is non-negotiable.

05
IPA Panel Wipe & Surface Preparation

Every panel is wiped with isopropyl alcohol (IPA) solution — removing all polishing oils, residues and fingerprints. The paint surface is left chemically clean and primed for maximum graphene oxide bond strength.

06
Graphene Coating Application & Controlled Curing

Graphene coating applied panel by panel using professional applicator blocks in cross-hatch motions, levelled with high-grade microfibre. Car is then placed in a controlled temperature and humidity environment for 24–48 hours to ensure the strongest possible graphene-paint bond.
