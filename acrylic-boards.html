About the Product

WHAT ARE ACRYLIC BOARDS?

Acrylic boards — also known as PMMA boards, plexiglass panels, or acrylic sheets — are rigid, crystal-clear or coloured plastic panels made from polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA). The material is prized for its exceptional optical clarity (clearer than standard glass), weather resistance, UV stability, and outstanding versatility in print and fabrication.

In the context of signage and business display, acrylic boards are used to create everything from illuminated shop front name boards to precision-engraved office nameplates — and everything in between.

At DRK Stickers, we design, cut, print, engrave, and assemble acrylic boards to your exact specifications — delivering and installing across Coimbatore within 2 to 7 working days depending on the project.

Why Acrylic Beats Everything Else

ACRYLIC VS FLEX BOARDS

Flex boards fade, crack in heat, and sag in rain within months. Acrylic holds its colour, shape, and clarity for 5–10 years.

✓ Acrylic wins: longevity

ACRYLIC VS WOODEN BOARDS

Wood warps in Coimbatore's monsoon humidity. Acrylic is moisture-resistant, needs zero maintenance, and looks cleaner.

✓ Acrylic wins: weather resistance

ACRYLIC VS METAL BOARDS

Metal is heavy, expensive, and hard to modify. Acrylic is lightweight, more affordable, and allows full-colour UV printing.

✓ Acrylic wins: design flexibility

ACRYLIC VS REGULAR GLASS

Acrylic is up to 17× stronger than glass, significantly lighter, and equally clear. It can be cut to any shape without specialist tools.

✓ Acrylic wins: strength & safety
