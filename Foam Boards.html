Questions answered
Frequently asked questions
What is the price of foam boards in Coimbatore?
Foam board pricing depends on size, material (standard or PVC), thickness, quantity, and finish. Small boards start from very affordable single-unit pricing. Bulk orders reduce the cost per board significantly. Contact DRK Stickers with your exact size and quantity for a free, instant quote.
Can you print foam boards on the same day?
For standard sizes and designs, many orders are completed within 24 hours. Same-day printing is available for urgent requirements depending on order size and current capacity. WhatsApp us your requirement directly to confirm availability for your deadline.
What file format should I send for printing?
We accept PDF, JPEG, PNG, and AI files. For best print results, provide artwork at 150 DPI minimum at full print size. CMYK colour mode is recommended for colour accuracy. If you're unsure, share your file and we'll check it for you before printing.
Can you cut foam boards into custom shapes?
Yes — we cut foam boards into any shape: rectangles with rounded corners, arches, circles, ovals, or fully custom silhouettes. Custom shape cutting is available for display standees, event décor props, and exhibition cutouts.
Can foam boards be used outdoors?
Standard paper-faced foam boards are designed for indoor use — outdoor rain and sunlight will damage them quickly. For semi-outdoor or shaded outdoor use, PVC foam board with UV printing is a more suitable option. Tell us your display environment and we'll recommend the right material.
How long do foam boards last?
Indoor paper-faced foam boards typically last 2–3 years when kept away from direct sunlight and moisture. PVC foam boards last longer and resist humidity and handling. For long-term use, PVC foam board with UV printing and laminate coating is recommended.
Do you provide design support?
Yes. Our in-house design team at DRK Stickers can create your foam board artwork from your content, logo, and brief. Share your text, images, and preferred layout — we produce a print-ready design for your approval before printing begins.
