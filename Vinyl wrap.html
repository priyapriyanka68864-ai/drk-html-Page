What is it?
Vehicle Vinyl Wrap — Explained

A vehicle vinyl wrap is a high-quality adhesive film applied directly over your car's original paint. It changes colour, adds custom designs, or gives a brand-new finish — without permanently altering the factory paintwork.

100% Reversible

Remove the wrap anytime and restore your factory paint — perfect for leased or luxury vehicles.

Paint Protection

The vinyl layer shields your original paint from UV rays, minor scratches, bird droppings, and dust.

Unlimited Designs

Choose from matte, gloss, satin, chrome, carbon fibre, or fully custom printed vehicle vinyl wrap designs.

Cost-Effective

A fraction of the cost of a full repaint — with more flexibility, faster completion, and better protection.

Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a vehicle vinyl wrap last in Coimbatore's climate?
A professionally installed, high-quality vinyl wrap typically lasts 3 to 5 years. Longevity depends on sun exposure, washing frequency, and usage conditions. Coimbatore's warm climate means UV-resistant premium films are especially recommended.
Will vinyl wrap damage my car's original paint?
No. When installed and removed by professionals, vinyl wraps do not damage factory paint. In fact, they protect it from UV rays, minor abrasions, and environmental elements during the wrap's lifetime.
How much does a car wrap cost in Coimbatore?
The cost depends on the wrap type (full or partial), finish chosen (matte, gloss, chrome), design complexity, and vehicle size. Contact DRK Stickers for a free, customised quote with no hidden charges.
Can I wash my wrapped car normally?
Yes — hand washing is recommended. Avoid high-pressure jet washes directly on wrap edges and do not use abrasive or chemical cleaners. A gentle car wash soap and microfibre cloth work best.
How long does the installation take?
A standard full-body colour change wrap takes 2 to 3 days. Partial wraps and single-panel jobs can be completed in just a few hours, depending on the vehicle and design complexity.
Can vinyl wrap be applied to any vehicle?
Yes — vinyl wraps work on cars, SUVs, bikes, vans, trucks, and commercial vehicles of all makes and models, including hatchbacks, sedans, and luxury cars.
