Why laser cutting
The precision advantage

Unmatched precision

CO₂ laser cutting achieves ±0.1mm accuracy. Intricate jali patterns, fine letterforms, and detailed artwork reproduced exactly — every single time.

Smooth, polished edges

The laser seals the cut edge as it works — producing naturally smooth, polished finishes on acrylic and clean sealed edges on MDF. No sanding, no finishing needed.

Any design complexity

Any design drawable on a computer can be laser cut. Complex mesh jali, calligraphy, logo cutouts — design complexity doesn't increase the cost.

Consistent batch production

Once your file is set, every piece comes out identical. Perfect for name board series, return gift sets, signage batches, and production runs of any size.

Minimal material waste

Computer-optimised cutting paths reduce material wastage significantly — keeping your cost per piece lower than manual cutting methods.

Zero contact on material

The laser never touches the material physically — no pressure, no vibration, no risk of warping or cracking delicate sheets during cutting.

Questions answered
Frequently asked questions
How much does laser cutting cost in Coimbatore?
Laser cutting pricing depends on the material, thickness, design complexity, area to be cut, and quantity. Acrylic and MDF cutting starts from ₹50–₹100 per square foot for simple cuts, with intricate jali patterns priced higher based on cutting time. Contact DRK Stickers with your material, size, and design for a free, accurate quote.
What is the maximum thickness you can laser cut?
Capacity depends on the material: acrylic up to 20mm, MDF up to 18mm, sunboard and PVC up to 10mm, plywood up to 12mm, and leather up to 5mm. Share your specific material and thickness and we'll confirm cutting capability for your project.
Can you laser cut very intricate jali patterns?
Yes — laser cutting is the ideal method for intricate jali and decorative mesh patterns. Our CO₂ laser achieves ±0.1mm accuracy, making it possible to cut patterns with very fine bridges, complex geometric detail, and tight curves consistently across multiple pieces.
Do I need to send a design file or can you create it?
Both options work. If you have a DXF, SVG, AI, PDF, or CDR file, we can proceed directly. If you don't have a file, share a reference image, sketch, or description — our in-house design team prepares a cutting-ready file for your approval before production begins.
How long does laser cutting take?
Simple cuts on standard sizes are typically completed within 24 hours. Complex intricate designs, large boards, or high-volume batch orders take 2–3 days. Contact us with your deadline and quantity and we'll confirm the schedule upfront.
What is the difference between laser cutting and CNC routing?
Laser cutting uses a focused beam for very fine, precise cuts — ideal for thin materials, intricate patterns, and smooth edges. CNC routing uses a spinning bit for thicker materials and wider cuts. For acrylic, MDF jali, and fine detail work, laser cutting produces significantly superior edge quality and detail accuracy.
Can you engrave as well as cut in the same job?
Yes — our CO₂ laser engraves logos, text, textures, and photographs on acrylic, wood, MDF, and leather with permanent, high-contrast results. Cutting and engraving can be combined in a single machine run, saving both time and cost on your order.
