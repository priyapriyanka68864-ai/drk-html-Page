Understanding the Service
What is Ceramic Coating
for Cars?

Ceramic coating — also called nano coating or glass coating — is a liquid polymer applied to your car's exterior paint by hand. When applied by a trained professional, it chemically bonds with the factory paint — becoming a permanent part of the surface rather than sitting on top like wax.

Once cured, the coating forms an ultra-hard, crystal-clear protective layer with two defining characteristics that make it superior to any other paint protection product available:

💎9H Hardness — Scratch Resistance

Professional ceramic coatings reach 9H hardness — the hardest rating on the pencil scale. This protects your paint against light scratches, swirl marks, bird droppings, tree sap, road grime and environmental contaminants.

🌊Hydrophobicity — Self-Cleaning

The ceramic layer creates an ultra-smooth, hydrophobic surface — water beads instantly and rolls off taking dirt with it. Your car stays cleaner longer and every wash is safer and dramatically easier.

Why Professional Application Matters

At DRK Stickers, we apply only professional-grade nano ceramic coatings — not off-the-shelf consumer products. The difference in bond strength, durability and gloss enhancement is significant and immediately visible.

Consumer-grade ceramic coatings sold online lack the molecular concentration and bond permanence of professional products — and without proper paint correction before application, even professional coatings will underperform. Our certified technicians ensure every step is done correctly.

The Process
Our Ceramic Coating
Application Process

A professional ceramic coating application is a multi-step precision process — not a quick spray and wipe. At DRK Stickers, every step is done correctly so the coating bonds for maximum performance and longevity.

01
Paint Inspection & Assessment

We inspect your car's paint under controlled lighting — identifying swirl marks, scratches, water spots, oxidation and all defects. We document the condition before work begins and discuss our recommendations with you.

02
Thorough Wash & Foam Decontamination

A full foam pre-wash and hand wash removes all surface dirt, dust and loose contaminants. This step is critical — any dirt trapped under the ceramic coating will remain there permanently after curing.

03
Clay Bar & Chemical Decontamination

Even after washing, paint holds embedded contaminants — brake dust, industrial fallout and iron particles — invisible to the naked eye. We perform a full clay bar treatment and chemical decontamination to completely clean the paint to its base level.

04
Paint Correction — Machine Polishing

The most critical step. All swirl marks, light scratches and defects are removed using professional machine polishers. The coating locks in whatever condition the paint is in — so paint correction before coating is essential for a perfect result.

05
Panel Wipe & IPA Preparation

After polishing, every panel is wiped with isopropyl alcohol (IPA) solution to remove all polish residue and oils — ensuring the paint surface is completely clean, bare and chemically ready to accept the ceramic coating bond.

06
Ceramic Coating Application & Curing

Coating is applied panel by panel using professional applicator pads in cross-hatch patterns, then levelled and allowed to flash — forming its chemical bond. The car is cured in a controlled environment for 24–48 hours before final inspection and delivery.
