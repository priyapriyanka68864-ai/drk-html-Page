What is it?
What is Decorative Window Film
— and How is it Different?

Decorative and frosted window film is a specially manufactured adhesive film applied to the surface of existing glass — designed to add visual patterns, privacy, texture effects and design interest to plain glass surfaces without any permanent modification, acid treatment or glass replacement.

Unlike acid etching or sandblasting which permanently alter the glass, decorative film from DRK Stickers achieves the exact same visual effect at a fraction of the cost — with the freedom to remove or change the design in the future.

💡 Key difference from other films: Frosted and decorative films provide privacy day AND night — unlike reflective sun control films that only work in daytime. The film blocks visibility in both directions at all times, whether it is bright outside or your interior lights are on at night.

All film edges are neatly trimmed and finished for a clean, professional appearance. Every glass panel is inspected under good lighting before sign-off. The film typically fully cures within 3–5 days — minor haziness during this period clears completely as the film bonds to the glass surface.
