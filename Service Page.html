Frequently Asked Questions
For a brand new car, we recommend starting with Paint Protection Film (PPF) to physically protect the paint from rock chips and scratches from day one. You can then add ceramic coating on top for UV and chemical protection. Our experts guide you during the free consultation.
For cars driven daily, we recommend a basic detail every 3–4 months and a full interior and exterior detail at least once a year. Cars with ceramic coating need maintenance washes every 2–3 months.
Ceramic coating is a nano-coating that bonds with paint — providing UV protection, hydrophobic properties and a high-gloss finish. PPF is a physical film that protects against rock chips and deep scratches. Many owners choose both — PPF underneath with ceramic coating on top for complete protection.
