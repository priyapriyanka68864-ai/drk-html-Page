Our Story

Was Born

DRK Stickers started with a simple belief — that Coimbatore's car owners deserved a better, more professional car care experience than what was available at the time.

When our founder first entered the car care industry, most local shops offered basic washes with low-quality products and zero attention to detail. There was no studio in Coimbatore that truly understood paint science, surface protection or the long-term care a car needs.

The Problem We Solved
Why We Started

Before DRK Stickers, car owners in Coimbatore had very few reliable options for professional car care. Most local shops used harsh chemicals, untrained staff and cheap products — leaving cars looking dull and poorly maintained within weeks. Paint correction was unheard of. Ceramic coating was a luxury only big-city studios offered. PPF was something people read about online but couldn't find locally.

We saw this problem clearly — and we built DRK Stickers to solve it. We invested in professional training, sourced premium international products, built a proper studio and hired technicians who are passionate about cars. We brought big-city level car detailing expertise right here to Coimbatore — at prices that are fair, transparent and accessible to every car owner. That is why hundreds of car owners in Coimbatore now trust DRK Stickers as the best detailing service in Coimbatore.
