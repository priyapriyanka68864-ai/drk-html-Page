The problem
Why factory seats fall short

Stains & fading fast

Factory cloth absorbs sweat, stains easily, and fades under Coimbatore's intense sun — looking tired within just a few years of daily use.

Poor comfort support

Stock seats offer minimal lumbar and thigh support. Upgraded upholstery with foam padding transforms long drives on Coimbatore's highways.

Lost resale value

Worn or damaged seat fabric significantly reduces your car's resale price. Quality seat covers protect the original upholstery underneath.

Heat discomfort

Dark factory seats absorb heat heavily in Coimbatore's climate. Breathable and ventilated leather options regulate cabin temperature far better.

Frequently asked questions
How much do leather seat covers cost in Coimbatore?
+
Entry-level art leather or PU seat covers start from ₹3,500–₹6,000 per full set. Mid-range Nappa leather covers range from ₹7,000–₹15,000. Full genuine leather or custom-stitched upholstery starts from ₹18,000 upward depending on vehicle and design complexity. Contact DRK Stickers for a free, itemised quote.
Will seat covers affect my car's side airbags?
+
No. All seat covers at DRK Stickers are stitched with airbag-compatible seams that allow side airbags to deploy fully and safely. Your factory safety systems are never compromised — this is a standard we apply to every installation.
How long does seat cover installation take?
+
A standard full-set seat cover installation (front and rear) takes 4–6 hours. Full re-upholstery with foam restoration and custom stitching takes 1–2 days. We'll give you an exact timeline at consultation based on your chosen service.
Can you install covers on any car model?
+
Yes — we work on all makes and models including Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Tata, Honda, Toyota, Kia, MG, Mahindra, Volkswagen, and more. Both compact hatchbacks and large SUVs are welcome at DRK Stickers.
How do I care for my leather seat covers?
+
Wipe with a slightly damp microfibre cloth for daily cleaning. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners or direct steam. For Nappa or genuine leather, use a leather conditioner every 3–6 months to keep the material supple and prevent cracking — especially important in Coimbatore's dry summer months.
Can I choose my own colour and stitch pattern?
+
Absolutely. DRK Stickers offers complete customisation — dual-tone colours, contrast stitching, diamond quilting, perforation patterns, and headrest embroidery. We work with you to create a design unique to your car before a single stitch is made.
