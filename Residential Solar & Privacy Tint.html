About the Service

What is Residential Solar & Privacy Film?

Residential solar film — also called solar control window film or sun control film — is a thin, optically clear or lightly tinted polyester laminate applied to the interior surface of your home's glass windows and doors.

The film contains multiple micro-thin layers of metals, ceramics, or dyes that work together to:

  • Block solar heat before it enters your room — reducing Solar Heat Gain
  • Filter 99% of UV rays that cause skin damage and fade furniture
  • Reduce glare from direct sunlight on screens and eyes by up to 83%
  • Add daytime privacy — one-way visibility so you see out but others can't see in
  • Insulate your glass — keeping cool air in during summer

No new windows, no structural work. Applied to your existing glass in a single day and lasting 10–15 years with proper care.
