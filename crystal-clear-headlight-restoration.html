About the Service

What is Headlight Restoration?

Headlight restoration is a professional process that removes yellowing, fogging, and oxidation from your car's plastic headlight lenses — restoring them to a clear, bright, like-new condition without replacing the entire headlight assembly.

Modern headlights are made from polycarbonate plastic protected by a factory UV coating. Coimbatore's intense year-round sunlight rapidly breaks down that coating, exposing the plastic to UV radiation, heat, and road grime. The result: lenses that look dull, yellow, or milky — and perform dangerously worse at night.

At DRK Stickers, we use professional-grade multi-stage wet sanding, machine polishing compounds, and a UV-protective sealant coat to bring back full clarity — at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

Why headlights go yellow

UV Oxidation — #1 Cause

Coimbatore's intense sunlight breaks down polycarbonate plastic at the molecular level. About 80% of all headlight fogging is UV-related.

Road Debris & Micro-scratches

Dust, gravel, and grit physically pit the lens surface. These micro-scratches scatter light, creating a frosted, hazy look.

Heat from Bulbs

Your headlights' own heat slowly degrades the plastic from the inside, contributing to discoloration and fogging over time.

Monsoon Moisture

Coimbatore's monsoon season pushes moisture into lens imperfections — accelerating cloudiness and internal fogging.

Foggy Headlights Are a Serious Safety Risk

Studies show that severely yellowed headlights produce as little as 20% of the light output of new headlights. That means your car is running on one-fifth of its designed visibility at night — giving you far less time to react to pedestrians, animals, and obstacles on the road.

On dark stretches around Coimbatore — towards Mettupalayam, Pollachi, or Tiruppur — this reduction in visibility is a genuine accident risk. Professional headlight restoration can recover up to 70% of your original light output.
